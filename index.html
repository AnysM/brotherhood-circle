The Intention

Something has
been erased

The knowledge of how to be a man among men. How to sit with fire. How to move through forest. How to speak truth in a circle of brothers.

For generations, this wasn't taught. It was lived.

This gathering is an attempt to live it again.

"Once you're through the other side, it's beautiful. Take the leap."
— Joubin  ·  His third Brotherhood Circle
Brotherhood

Why gather
as brothers

There are things that only happen in the company of other men. Not competition. Not performance.

Accountability. Honesty. The kind of silence that holds you. Brotherhood is not a concept. It's a practice. And it changes you.

This week is a rite of passage — an initiatory process where each man sheds what no longer serves him and remembers what he's made of.

The Forest

After the desert,
the forest

Our first gatherings took us to the desert of Morocco — wide open, stripped back, ancestral.

This time, the forest is calling. The lakes, the pines, the cold water, the dark earth of Northern Europe.

Our own roots. What our ancestors knew before it was forgotten. Nature doesn't ask who you are. It reminds you.

The Programme

Eight days in the forest

To reconnect with what is alive and wild in us, and carry it back into the world with our brothers.

Stone Circles

The Venue

Silent Lake,
North Poland

One of the biggest forest complexes in Europe. A land off the grid, off the road.

Built and tended with care by Adam, its guardian, for over a decade. A temazcal by the water. A sauna. Ancient trees. Total stillness.

Not a retreat centre. A living place.
