Remembering

Something has
been forgotten

The knowledge of how to be a man among men. How to sit with fire to reflect. How to move through life effortlessly. How to speak truth from the heart.

For generations, this wasn't taught. It was lived.

Now, we are trying to remember what was already inside of us.

"Once you're through the other side, it's beautiful. Take the leap."
— Joubin  ·  His third Brotherhood Circle
Brotherhood

Why gather
as brothers

There are things that only happen in the company of other men.

Not competition. Not performance.

Accountability. Honesty. The kind of silence that holds you.

Brotherhood is not a concept. It's a practice. And it changes you.

This week is a rite of passage — an initiatory process where each man sheds what no longer serves him and finds what he's made of.

The Forest

In the wilderness

After the desert of Morocco, this time, the forest is calling.

The lakes, the pines, the cold water, the dark earth of Northern Europe.

Going back to primality. To what our ancestors knew before it was forgotten.

Nature doesn't ask who you are. It reminds you.

The Programme

A week of initiation

Eight days in the forest. To reconnect with what is alive and wild in us, and carry it back into the world.

Temazcal
Sweat ceremony by the water. Purification through fire and steam
Mongolian Throat Singing

Deer Walk
Moving through the forest in silence and presence
Stone Circles
Ancient ritual structure, collective intention setting
Bushcraft

Sauna

About Brotherhood Circle

A global movement
rooted in men's work

Who comes

  • Men between 28 and 50, from Europe and beyond
  • Those who feel something essential is missing
  • Men going through transitions — fatherhood, burnout, loss
  • Those already exploring therapy, meditation, or men's work
  • Anyone tired of pretending and ready to go deeper

What we stand for

  • Brotherhood — real, honest, without performance
  • Ancestral wisdom brought into modern life
  • Nature as a teacher, not just a backdrop
  • Integrity — we walk our talk
  • Sovereignty — each man is his own authority
Our Approach
01
Immersion in Ancient Cultures

We travel to places where traditional knowledge is still alive — learning directly from rituals, stories, and ways of living.

02
Men's Circle & Inner Work

We sit in circle as equals — breathwork, embodiment, storytelling — exploring identity, purpose, and gifts.

03
Integration

Not an escape from reality — a preparation to re-enter it with more clarity. Every man brings something. Every man leaves changed.
